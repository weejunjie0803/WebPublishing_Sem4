NASI LEMAK

Plain white rice is steamed in coconut milk; Coconut milk, or santan, is the “lemak” of the dish: the richness.

NASI LEMAK iN ART

MADE BY : ERIC S | GRAPHIC DESIGNER

The nasi lemak started off as a farmer’s meal. Long days in the field meant a filling dish ready for on-the-go eating was essential. The rice, oils, and fish provided a balanced diet in one packet, and it was easily concoct too. A recipe of Malay origin, it made use of the seafront harvests, coconuts, and fish.
